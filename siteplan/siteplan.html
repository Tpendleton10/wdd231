FitPulse

Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

FitPulse

Domain: fitpulse.com

Reasoning: The name "FitPulse" was selected because it captures the essence of fitness and vitality. "Pulse" represents the heartbeat of health and fitness, suggesting both energy and the monitoring aspect of fitness tracking. The name is short, memorable, and immediately conveys that this is a dynamic fitness platform that keeps users connected to their health goals.

Site Purpose

FitPulse serves as your ultimate fitness companion, providing a comprehensive platform for fitness enthusiasts to track, monitor, and achieve their health goals. The site provides:

  • BMI Calculator: Quick and accurate Body Mass Index calculations with health category classifications
  • Workout Library: Extensive database of exercises with detailed instructions and demonstrations
  • Progress Tracking: Tools to log workouts, track improvements, and monitor fitness milestones
  • Nutrition Guidance: Calorie tracking, meal planning, and nutritional information
  • Fitness Articles: Educational content on workout techniques, nutrition tips, and wellness advice
  • Goal Setting: Personalized fitness objectives with progress monitoring
  • Community Features: User profiles, achievements, and motivational content

The platform is designed for fitness enthusiasts of all levels, from beginners starting their fitness journey to advanced athletes looking to optimize their performance and track their progress systematically.

Target Audience Scenarios

Scenario 1: Health-Conscious Beginner

Question: "I want to start getting fit but don't know where to begin. Can I check if my current weight is healthy and find beginner-friendly workouts?"

Site Response: FitPulse provides an easy-to-use BMI calculator to assess current health status, along with a curated library of beginner workouts with clear instructions and progress tracking features.

Scenario 2: Busy Professional

Question: "I have limited time to exercise. Can I find quick, effective workouts that I can do at home or in a small space?"

Site Response: The workout library includes time-efficient routines, home workouts, and bodyweight exercises that can be filtered by duration and equipment needed.

Scenario 3: Fitness Enthusiast

Question: "I've been working out for a while and want to track my progress and try new exercises. How can I log my workouts and discover new routines?"

Site Response: Advanced workout tracking tools, progress charts, and an extensive exercise database with detailed instructions for trying new fitness challenges.

Scenario 4: Weight Management Seeker

Question: "I want to lose weight and need to understand my current health status and track my nutrition alongside my workouts."

Site Response: BMI tracking with health recommendations, calorie counters, and integrated workout and nutrition logging for comprehensive weight management.

Color Schema

Electric Blue (#007bff)

Usage: Primary navigation, main headings, call-to-action buttons, and brand elements. This vibrant blue conveys energy, trust, and motivation - perfect for a fitness platform.

Vibrant Green (#28a745)

Usage: Success indicators, progress bars, achievement badges, and positive feedback. Green represents health, growth, and accomplishment.

Energy Orange (#fd7e14)

Usage: Secondary buttons, highlights, workout intensity indicators, and motivational elements. Orange conveys energy and enthusiasm.

Deep Gray (#343a40)

Usage: Body text, secondary headings, and content areas. Provides excellent readability and professional appearance.

Light Background (#f8f9fa)

Usage: Page backgrounds, card backgrounds, and subtle sections. Creates clean spacing and modern visual hierarchy.

Typography

Primary Font: Roboto

Usage: All headings (H1-H6), navigation items, and button text

Characteristics: Modern, clean sans-serif font that's highly legible and conveys professionalism and reliability
